Submission Roadmap

Welcome to the Cozulemez Music Group (CMG) official legal documentation. This section covers the terms of our digital operations and the professional standards of our partnership procurement.

1. Demo Submissions & A&R Strategy

At CMG, we maintain a highly selective catalog to ensure every artist receives the strategic depth required for global growth. Our submission process is the first step in identifying long-term partners.

The Submission Lifecycle

Every demo submitted via our portal undergoes a rigorous 48-hour evaluation phase. We don't just listen; we analyze the project's ecosystem.

  • Step 1: Fidelity Audit: Our engineers check the mix/master quality to ensure it meets DSP broadcast standards (Loudness, Dynamic Range).
  • Step 2: Brand Cohesion: We review your social footprint and visual assets. A strong artist needs a story, not just a song.
  • Step 3: Territory Potential: We identify which of our global hubs (Frankfurt, London, Istanbul, LA) aligns best with your sound.
  • Step 4: Final Curatorial Vote: The A&R board makes a unanimous decision on partnership offers.

How To Be Selected?

We receive thousands of submissions. To stand out, ensure your submission includes:

  • Unreleased Clarity: We prefer demos that are 2-3 months away from release.
  • Marketing Vision: Briefly describe how you see this song living in the digital space.
  • Professionalism: High-resolution artwork and a clear, concise artist bio.

2. Intellectual Property & Ownership

The CMG distribution model is built on the principle of equity. We provide the infrastructure; you remain the owner. All masters, creative assets, and performance rights remain the property of the creator or the label partner.

By using our platform, you grant CMG the limited right to distribute and promote your content across digital and physical channels as defined in your specific partnership agreement.

3. Terms of Digital Interaction

Accessing this portal implies acceptance of our professional conduct standards. CMG reserves the right to terminate interaction or access for projects that do not meet our ethical or quality guidelines.

All data presented on this site (Technology previews, PR strategies) is the proprietary information of CMG and is protected by international copyright law.

4. Global Rights & Disclaimer

CMG provides world-class distribution infrastructure "as is." While we facilitate direct access to global DSPs, the success of a release is a shared responsibility between the creator's vision and CMG's strategic execution.
