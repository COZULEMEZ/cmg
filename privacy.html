Privacy Policy

At Cozulemez Music Group (CMG), we prioritize the security and confidentiality of creator data. This policy explains how we handle your professional information.

1. Data Collection & Artist Intelligence

When you submit a demo or join the CMG distribution network, we collect data including identity (Legal/Stage Name), email, social media metrics, and music metadata. This data is used exclusively for evaluating partnership potential and optimizing release strategies.

2. Third-Party Integration

As a global distributor, your data may be shared with Digital Service Providers (Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube, etc.) and physical partners solely for the purpose of distribution and promotion. We do not sell your data to marketing agencies or unauthorized third parties.

3. Metadata Integrity & Analytics

The analytics provided through the CMG Backoffice are derived from raw performance data. This information is processed securely to provide you with accurate revenue reports and territory insights.

4. Security Protocol

We implement world-class technical safeguards to protect your music assets and financial information. Artist portals are encrypted, and access is restricted to authorized personnels within the CMG management team.

5. Your Rights

Under global privacy regulations, you have the right to request access to your data, modification of metadata, or deletion of your interaction history with the CMG platform.
